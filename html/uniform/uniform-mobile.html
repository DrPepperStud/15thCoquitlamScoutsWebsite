Cub Scout Uniform

The uniform for the Cub section consists of the following:

  • Gray Cub Scout uniform shirt
  • Navy blue uniform pants
  • Neckerchief with Neckerchief slide
  • Black socks
  • Black shoes
  • The Scouts Canada hat (optional)

The following photo shows what the uniform should look like. Do note that the color of the neckerchief for our group is brown and gray instead of red (as neckerchief color is varies by group).

Scout Uniform

The uniform for the Scout section consists of the following:

  • Green Scouts uniform shirt, tucked in
  • Tan Scouts uniform pants
  • Brown belt
  • Neckerchief with Neckerchief slide
  • Black socks
  • Black shoes
  • The Scouts Canada hat (optional)

The following photo shows what the uniform should look like. Do note that the color of the neckerchief for our group is brown and gray instead of tan (as neckerchief color is varies by group).
