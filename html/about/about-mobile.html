Who are we?

The 15th Coquitlam Scout Group is a passionate youth group that strives on delivering a program that helps develop youth into the leaders of the future. Our program includes many outdoor activities such as hiking, camping, canoeing, rock-climbing and paddleboarding.

Programs

Due to COVID-19, we currently have reduced our programs to just the Cubs (age 8-10) and the Scouts (age 11-14), for the time being.

Meetings

Our weekly meetings are held at Pinetree Elementary School at 7PM on Thursdays. Curious about what our meetings are like? Stop by for a visit!
